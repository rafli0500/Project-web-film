Kupu-Kupu Kertas

Tahun Rilis: 2024

Penerbit: Maxima pictures

"Kupu Kupu Kertas" tells the story of Ning, daughter of a PKI leader, and Ihsan, a young man from a Nahdlatul Ulama family, whose love is tested by the political turmoil of 1965 Indonesia. The film portrays their love story against the backdrop of escalating violence and ideological conflict, highlighting the tragic consequences of the 1965 events in Banyuwangi. .
